About me

Hi, my name is Jan and I'm a half German half British Web developer born in 1989 in Nuremberg, Germany. I currently work as a Senior Software Engineer at MatchManao where I am responsible for the whole software stack for our platform and the platform itself. In my spare time I mentor anybody who wants to try their hand at programming or who wants to get better at programming — so if you're interested drop me a line at me [at] janhohner [dot] de, on Mastodon or on Twitter. I'm always happy to help, especially if you're just getting started.
